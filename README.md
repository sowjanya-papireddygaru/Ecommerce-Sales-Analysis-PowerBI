# Sales-Analysis-PowerBI
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing yearly sales performance, net revenue, and ROI using KPIs, with insights on discounts and marketing effectiveness.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze revenue generated by each product category over the year  
- Perform customer segmentation based on purchasing behavior and discount usage (e.g., regular vs occasional customers)  
- Identify monthly revenue trends to understand seasonal patterns  
- Evaluate the relationship between marketing spend and revenue generated

---

## Key Insights
- The Sports category generated the highest revenue among all product categories, indicating strong customer demand  
- Revenue shows noticeable fluctuations across different months, suggesting the presence of seasonal trends in sales performance  
- Marketing spend is not directly proportional to revenue generation; higher spending does not always result in higher returns  
- Certain categories (e.g: Electronics) have higher marketing spend but do not generate proportional revenue, while others (e.g: Sports) perform better with relatively optimized spend  
- Sales performance varies across categories, highlighting the importance of focusing on high-performing products rather than uniformly increasing marketing efforts - Units sold and category demand play a key role in revenue generation, indicating that marketing strategies should prioritize high demand products

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing product categories such as Sports, Electronics, and Toys, especially during peak months where revenue is consistently higher  
- Align marketing strategies with seasonal trends by increasing promotions and product availability during high-revenue periods (mid-year and selected peak months like January, May–August)  
- Optimize marketing spend by prioritizing categories that generate higher returns (e.g: Electronics), and reassess spending in categories where high investment does not yield proportional revenue (e.g: Toys)  
- Adopt a targeted marketing approach by promoting high-demand products across emerging and effective channels, including new social media platforms    
- Analyze the decline in revenue from 2024 to 2025 and identify potential causes (e.g: reduced demand, ineffective campaigns, or market changes) to take corrective actions
